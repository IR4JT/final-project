Accelerating the World's Transition to Sustainable Energy

Tesla designs and manufactures electric vehicles, battery energy storage systems, solar energy products, and related software. Tesla aims to accelerate the world’s transition to sustainable energy through innovations in EVs, clean power generation, and autonomous driving technology.

The Challenge: Tesla’s mission includes deploying a global fleet of fully autonomous vehicles. As of April 2025, no official Robotaxi app exists. So, I set out to design a prototype for what such an app could look like. Focusing on user experience, functionality, and the seamless integration of self-driving technology into everyday life.

Brand Guidelines

To maintain brand consistency, I drew inspiration from Tesla’s existing app. I used their color palette, typography, and visual style as a foundation, adapting them to suit the unique functions and user flows of the Robotaxi experience.
