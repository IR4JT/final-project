Mezze AI

Designed an AI support bot for CAVA’s iPhone App. The bot assists users with common inquiries like menu items, allergens, and order support. While reflecting the brand’s warm and modern tone. I built user flows, designed the interface in Figma, and prototyped a conversation with overlay animations. The design emphasizes clarity, accessibility, and trust.
