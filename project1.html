Accelerating the world's transition to sustainable energy

Tesla designs and manufactures electric vehicles, battery energy storage systems from home to grid-scale, solar panels and solar roof tiles, and related software and hardware to accelerate the world's transition to sustainable energy through innovations in battery technology, powertrain, and autonomous driving technology.

The Challenge: To achieve a sustainable energy future, Tesla needs to drive innovation in battery technology, powertrain, and autonomous driving technology to create compelling, affordable, and widely adopted electric vehicles.

Challenge

Tesla's mission is to accelerate the world's transition to sustainable energy. To achieve this mission, Tesla is building products that replace fossil fuels and drive innovations in battery technology, powertrain, and autonomous driving technology.
